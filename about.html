About Skilloft

Our Mission

Skilloft was created to help students find internships, competitions, scholarships, and courses in one place. We believe students should have easy access to opportunities that help them build skills, strengthen college applications, and explore their interests.

What We Offer

• Prestigious competitions

• Scholarships from around the world

• Free and high-quality courses
